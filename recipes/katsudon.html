Katsu Don

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1 Cup Dashi
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Shoyu
  • 2 Tbsp Mirin

Others

  • 2 Tonkatsu
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 2 servings Rice

Steps

  1. Cook the onion in the savory broth (Dashi, Sugar, Shoyu, Mirin) till tender.
  2. Place Tonkatsu on top to let it absorb the flavors of the broth.
  3. Add the beaten egg mixture and cook till just set.
  4. Place on top of rice and enjoy!
