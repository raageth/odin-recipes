Gyu Don

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1/2 Cup Dashi
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 3 Tbsp Shoyu
  • 2 Tbsp Mirin
  • 2 Tbsp Sake

Others

  • 500g Beef
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 2 servings Rice

Steps

  1. Cook the onion in the savory broth (Dashi, Sugar, Shoyu, Mirin, Sake) till tender.
  2. Place Beef on top to let it absorb the flavors of the broth.
  3. Place on top of rice and enjoy!
